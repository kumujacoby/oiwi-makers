Week 1: 2d6 System Introduction

Goal: Introduce tabletop RPGs ‚Ä¢ Identify 3 student DM candidates

System: 2d6

Roll 2d6: 1-3 Failure, 4-9 Success, 10-12 Great Success

Hit Points: Everyone has 3 HP

Characters: Index cards - 5 minute creation

Materials Needed

  • 16-20 sets of 2d6
  • 100+ index cards
  • Markers and colored pencils
  • LEGO bins with minifig parts
  • Station signs for 4 groups
Day 1.1: Welcome & 2d6 Basics
0:00-0:10

Welcome & TTRPG Introduction

Tell stories together using imagination and dice.

Example: "You find a locked door. What do you do? Roll to see if it works!"

0:10-0:20

Teach 2d6 System

Write on board:

  • Roll 2 dice, add them together
  • 1-3 = Failure
  • 4-9 = Success (biggest range!)
  • 10-12 = Great Success

Everyone practices rolling once.

0:20-0:40

Character Creation

Format: "You are a [race] [class] named [name]. You like [trait] and have [item]."

Example: "You are an Elven wizard named Trixie. You like fire and have a jar of eyeballs."

Draw 3 circles on back for HP.

0:40-1:10

Demo Adventure: "The Friendly Ghost"

Hook: Ghost making noise in library at night.

Scene 1: Investigate (find floating books, hear crying)

Scene 2: Meet ghost (looks sad, not scary)

Scene 3: Help ghost find their lost book

Resolution: Ghost becomes library guardian

1:10-1:30

Wrap & Preview

Debrief: What was fun? Tomorrow: small groups + LEGO!

Day 1.2: Station Rotations Begin

Station System (20 min per station)

  • Station 1 (with Kumu): "The Lost Puppy" adventure
  • Station 2: Build LEGO character minifig
  • Station 3: Draw and decorate character card
  • Station 4: Dice practice - roll 10 times, track results
Day 1.3: More Adventures

Adventure: "The Cookie Thief" - someone stealing cookies from bakery

Follow crumbs, discover hungry creature, solve without violence.

Day 1.4: Team Adventure

Adventure: "The Bridge of Riddles" - talking bridge blocks path

Note: Identify top 5-6 DM candidates by end of day.

Day 1.5: Epic Finale & DM Selection

Adventure: "The Dragon's Birthday"

Select Student DMs

Pull aside 3 students: "Would you like to learn to run games?"

Week 2: ENI System & Student DM Training

Goal: Transition to ENI ‚Ä¢ Train student DMs ‚Ä¢ Form groups

System: ENI

Dice Pool: d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20 (one of each)

Roll 4+ to succeed ‚Ä¢ Spent dice unavailable until rest

Eni Currency: Silver = +1, Gold = re-roll

Materials Needed

  • 16 full polyhedral dice sets
  • ENI character sheets (printed)
  • ENI zines (4 copies)
  • Tokens for eni currency
  • Student DM notebooks (3)
Day 2.1: Introduce ENI (Whole Class)

Teach ENI System

"What if you could CHOOSE which dice to use?"

Show the 6 dice. Bigger = more powerful, but only ONE of each.

First ENI Adventure: "The Shrinking Spell"

Everything in wizard's tower slowly shrinking! Find spell book, reverse it.

Day 2.2: Student DM Practice

Each student DM runs SHORT (15 min) encounter.

Kumu rotates, coaches in real-time.

Day 2.3: Formalize Groups

Groups: Red, Blue, Green (each with student DM)

Start building shared LEGO home base locations.

Day 2.4: Student DMs Lead

3 tables running simultaneously. Student DMs facilitate.

Day 2.5: Mastery & Reflection

Showcase LEGO builds, share stories.

Preview: Next week YOU create adventures!

Week 3: Student-Created Adventures

Goal: Groups design and run their own ENI adventures

Adventure Structure
